Introduction

Our Flavor is an icon!

Our product Dr. Nut was born in New Orleans during the late 1930s, introducing joy, pride and flavor to The Big Easy! And now we're back, more original and tasteful than ever!

Our new identity brings together the energy, nostalgia and the classic feeling we all love on Dr. Nut with our new set of products: the original flavor, chocolate flavor and pistachio flavor.

Take a look below!
